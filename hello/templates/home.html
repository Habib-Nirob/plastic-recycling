THE WORLD’S LARGEST AND MOST TRUSTED SOURCE OF FAIR-TRADE VERIFIED RECYCLED PLASTIC

OUR PLANET

33 double-decker busloads of plastic waste are burned or dumped in developing countries every 30 seconds*

OUR PEOPLE

More than 10 million informal waste collectors across the world are marginalized and live in poverty.

A HUMANITARIAN SOLUTION FOR AN ENVIRONMENTAL CHALLENGE.

WE USE PLASTIC WASTE AS A RESOURCE TO CREATE BETTER LIVELIHOODS AND A CLEANER PLANET.

Responsible & Fair Supply Chains

Our ethical sourcing platform connects informal waste collectors to global markets, ensuring our brand partners a consistent supply of high quality recycled plastic through our responsible and transparent supply chains.

Offset & EPR for Brands

For brands that aren’t ready to source recycled plastic we provide offset and EPR services - helping them contribute to the recovery and recycling certified ocean-bound plastic and giving directly to those at the base of the supply chain..

WANT TO JOIN THE MOVEMENT?

Plastic Offset

Did you know that the average person in North America or Western Europe uses 100 kgs of plastic per year? Well today, by directly contributing to the recovery of ocean-bound plastic you can make a dent in the amount of plastic that you use and also create a better future for the waste collectors that are collecting it. You can even gift a plastic offset to your friends and family!.
